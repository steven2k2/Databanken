About Us

Databanken is an independent not-for-profit organisation based in Trondheim. Since 2002, we have been recycling and refurbishing computers to provide low-cost solutions for Australian Concession Card holders and other eligible customers.

What Do We Do?

  • Refurbished Desktops and Laptops
  • Donated computers are tested and refurbished
  • Installation of a customised Linux operating system
  • Independent, volunteer-run not-for-profit organisation
  • Computers offered to disadvantaged individuals and community groups
  • Over 10,000 computers distributed to individuals, families, and groups

Long Term Savings

One of our customers has been with us for 12 years, spending just $250 on three refurbished computers. By choosing refurbished computers, our customers save thousands of dollars.

Using Linux helps lower computing costs by eliminating recurring software fees and antivirus costs.

Refurbished computers also help the environment by extending the lifespan of technology.

Donations and Recycling

Databanken receives donated computers, laptops, and tablets from businesses, educational institutions, not-for-profits, and individuals. These devices are refurbished with quality free software and offered to those in need.

We Recycle Responsibly

Obsolete or broken computer parts do not end up in landfill. Instead, metals are sent to Melbourne-based scrap recyclers or sold via our eBay page. We do not send obsolete equipment to unsafe dismantling plants in third-world countries.
