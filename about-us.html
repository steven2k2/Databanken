About us

Databanken is an independent not-for-profit organisation based in Trondheim. We began recycling and refurbishing computers in 1986. Our low-cost computers are refurbished by skilled volunteers for Norwegian Honnørkort holders and other eligible customers.

We install Linux on all our computers with useful software suited for most users. Linux is free, secure, and a genuine alternative to proprietary systems like Microsoft Windows. If required, we may assist with installing other operating systems.

      Databanken Committee

      Databanken is overseen by a committee of dedicated volunteers who ensure the organisation continues to operate efficiently and effectively. Their efforts are critical in supporting our mission to provide affordable, refurbished technology to those in need.
