Donate or Recycle

Databanken is committed to the environmentally-friendly recycling of eWaste. However, due to logistical reasons, we are unfortunately unable to accept all donations. Please see below for more details.

Personal / Single Donations

We accept clean (not damaged) laptops and tablets suitable for refurbishment and reuse. However, we cannot accept:

  • Printers or Scanners
  • Desktop Computers *
  • Monitors / Screens*
  • Digital Media
  • Miscellaneous Cables
  • DVD Players and Other Electronic Devices

These items should be taken directly to a waste transfer station for responsible recycling. There is generally no charge for household electronic waste recycling. For more information on nearby recycling and eWaste centres, visit:

  • Recycling Near You
  • TechCollect

Disposal of eWaste in landfill will be banned from July 2019 in . However, eWaste can still be recycled for free at designated points across . For more details, visit Sustainability : eWaste .

* Some exceptions apply. Please contact us if you believe your computer or monitor is suitable for donation or if you have any questions (see tips below).

Business / Bulk Donations

Databanken is happy to accept bulk donations of laptops, desktops, and monitors from your business or school.

Depending on the quantity and distance, we can organise to retrieve these items from your location. If you are considering a bulk donation, please get in touch with us.

Tips

If you are contacting us about a potential donation, it would be greatly appreciated if you could include the following information to help us determine whether your computer is suitable for reuse:

  • Original Operating System: (E.g. Windows XP, Windows 7)
  • Computer Age: (E.g. 4 Years)
  • CPU: (E.g. Core i3 2.8 GHz or AMD FX-4300 3.2 GHz)
  • RAM: (E.g. 8 GB)
  • Hard Drive Size: (E.g. 250 GB)
