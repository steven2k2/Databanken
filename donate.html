Donate or Recycle

Databanken is committed to the environmentally friendly recycling of e-waste. However, due to logistical reasons, we are unfortunately unable to accept all donations. Please see below for more details.

Personal / Single Donations

We accept clean (not damaged) laptops and tablets suitable for refurbishment and reuse. However, we cannot accept:

  • Printers or Scanners
  • Desktop Computers *
  • Monitors / Screens*
  • Digital Media
  • Miscellaneous Cables
  • DVD Players and Other Electronic Devices

Alternative Recycling Options

For responsible disposal, please visit:

  • Recycling Near You
  • TechCollect
  • Sustainability Victoria: eWaste

Business / Bulk Donations

We welcome bulk donations of laptops, desktops, and monitors from businesses or schools. Depending on quantity and location, we may arrange collection.

How to Proceed

Contact us to discuss collection and donation options.
