Donate or Recycle

Databanken is committed to environmentally friendly recycling of e-waste. However, due to logistical reasons, we are unfortunately unable to accept all donations. Please see below for more details.

Your donation helps provide affordable, refurbished computers to individuals and organisations in need, reducing eWaste and bridging the digital divide.

If you can’t donate equipment, consider making a monetary donation to help us continue our mission. Every contribution supports refurbishment costs and operations.

Personal / Single Donations

Laptop sales are increasingly popular, and demand for desktop computers is falling. Databanken is pleased to accept donations of all brands of clean (not damaged) laptops and tablets suitable for refurbishment and reuse. However, we are unable to accept:

  • Printers or Scanners
  • Desktop Computers *
  • Monitors / Screens *
  • Digital Media
  • Miscellaneous Cables
  • DVD Players and other Electronic Devices

These items should be taken directly to a waste transfer station for responsible recycling. There is generally no charge for household electronic waste recycling. You can find more information on nearby recycling/eWaste centres and resources at:

  • Recycling Near You
  • TechCollect

Disposal of eWaste in landfill will be banned from July 2019 in Victoria. However, eWaste is still recyclable for no charge at designated points across Australia. For more information on this change, please visit Sustainability Victoria: eWaste.

* Some exceptions apply: Please contact us if you believe your computer or monitor is suitable for donation or have any questions.

Business / Bulk Donations

Databanken is happy to accept bulk donations of laptops, desktops, and monitors from your business or school. Depending on the quantity and distance, we can organize to retrieve these items from your location. If you are considering making a bulk donation, please contact us.

Tips

If you are contacting us about a potential donation, it would be greatly appreciated if you could include the following information to help us assess if your computer can be given a second life:

  • Original Operating System: (e.g., Windows XP, Windows 7)
  • Computer Age: (e.g., 4 Years)
  • CPU: (e.g., Core i3 2.8 GHz or AMD FX-4300 3.2 GHz)
  • RAM: (e.g., 8 GB)
  • Hard Drive Size: (e.g., 250 GB)

Note: Depending on your operating system, this information may be found in different places. We suggest checking Google, for example, "View system specs Windows 10". Please contact us if you need help finding this information.

Before donating, please ensure your data is backed up and the device is wiped clean. For guidance on securely erasing your data, check online resources or contact us for assistance.
