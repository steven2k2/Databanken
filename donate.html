Donate or Recycle

Databanken is committed to the environmentally friendly recycling of e-waste. However, due to logistical reasons, we are unfortunately unable to accept all donations. Please see below for more details.

Personal / Single Donations

We accept clean (not damaged) laptops and tablets suitable for refurbishment and reuse. However, we cannot accept:

  • Printers or Scanners
  • Desktop Computers *
  • Monitors / Screens*
  • Digital Media
  • Miscellaneous Cables
  • DVD Players and Other Electronic Devices

These items should be taken directly to a waste transfer station for responsible recycling, there is generally no charge for household electronic waste recycling. You can find more information on nearby recycling / eWaste centres and resources at:

  • Recycling Near You
  • TechCollect

Disposal of eWaste in landfill will be banned from July 2019 in Trondelag; however, eWaste is still able to be recycled for no charge at designated points across Norway. For more information on this change please see Sustainability Trondelag: eWaste

*Some exceptions do apply, please contact us if you believe your computer or monitor is suitable for donation or you have any questions (see tips below).

Business / Bulk Donations

Databanken is happy to accept bulk donations of laptops, desktops and monitors from your business or school.

Depending on the quantity and distance we can organise to retrieve these items from your location, If you are considering making a bulk donation, please contact us
