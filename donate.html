Donate or Recycle

Databanken is committed to the environmentally friendly recycling of e-waste however due to logistical reasons we are unfortunately unable to accept all donations, please see below for more details.

Personal / Single Donations

Laptop sales are increasingly popular, and demand for desktops is falling. We accept clean (not damaged) laptops and tablets suitable for refurbishment and reuse. However, we cannot accept:

  • Printers or Scanners
  • Desktop Computers *
  • Monitors / Screens*
  • Digital Media
  • Miscellaneous Cables
  • DVD Players and other Electronic Devices

These items should be taken directly to a waste transfer station for responsible recycling. More information:

  • Recycling Near You
  • TechCollect
  • Sustainability Victoria: eWaste

*Some exceptions do apply, please contact us if you believe your computer or monitor is suitable for donation, or you have any questions (see tips below).

Business / Bulk Donations

Databanken is happy to accept bulk donations of laptops, desktops and monitors from your business or school.

Depending on quantity and distance we can organise to retrieve these items from your location, If you are considering making a bulk donation, please contact us. We may be able to arrange a collection depending on quantity and location.

Donation Tips

If you are contacting us about a donation, please include the following details:

  • Original Operating System (e.g., Windows XP, Windows 7)
  • Computer Age (e.g. 4 Years)
  • CPU (e.g., Core i3 2.8 GHz or AMD FX-4300 3.2 GHz)
  • RAM (e.g., 8 GB)
  • Hard Drive Size (e.g. 250 GB)

Note: Depending on your operating system, this information may be found in different places. We suggest checking Google, for example, View system specs Windows 10", please contact us if you need help finding this information.
