OUR MISSION

At Databanken, our mission is to bridge the digital divide by providing affordable and reliable computers to individuals and community groups in need. Through our efforts, we aim to empower people with access to technology and the tools they need to thrive in a digital world.

What We Do

  • Refurbish and distribute low-cost computers to eligible individuals and groups.
  • Equip systems with open-source software, providing cost-effective solutions for education, work, and personal use.
  • Offer training and support to help users maximize their potential with the technology we provide.

How We Help

By recycling and repurposing donated computers, we reduce electronic waste and provide sustainable technology solutions. Every computer distributed helps someone access education, employment opportunities, and community engagement.

Get Involved

Volunteer with us or donate your old computers to make a difference in your community.
