Databanken

A self-funded charity run by volunteers, Databanken is committed to bridging the "digital divide" and making computing accessible to those less fortunate in our society. For over 20 years, we have operated as a dedicated organisation. The style and technology of computing have changed dramatically, yet affordability remains a barrier for those less fortunate.

We are continually indebted to both our dedicated volunteers and loyal supporters as we strive to meet the ever-increasing demand for quality used systems that can be supplied to those in need at a minimal cost. This partnership has resulted in the reuse of over 10,000 systems that would otherwise have contributed to e-waste in landfill.

For more information on our mission and organisation, visit our About Us page. To learn more about the computers available at Databanken, visit the Our Computers section or browse from the menu above.

Databanken would not be possible without the generosity of our supporters. If you are interested in supporting us, please visit our Donate page for more information. If you are interested in volunteering, you can find details on our Volunteer Information page.
